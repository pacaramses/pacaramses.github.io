About Us

We have created this website in honour of Paca the alpaca plushie. We post about alpaca facts, Paca's memories and alpaca events!

Paca was born two years ago and made her appearance in Card Factory. Since then, she's been on many adventures around the globe, from Morocco to Poland. Her favourite subject is Biology, and she loves sleeping, playing and learning about the world.

Paca meets Antigen-Antibody complex.

By Sadia Amin

On Friday 30th August, Lord Paca met Antigen-Antibody complex for the first time.

Antigen-Antibody complex was given as a gift to the Paca family. Paca was excited to meet her new sibling for the first time. Upon their meeting, Paca was amazed by the flexibility of the Fab arms. Needless to say, she was impressed by the design. Paca is a scientist after all!
